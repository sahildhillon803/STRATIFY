## About Section Text
STRATIFY transforms disjointed startup financials into actionable intelligence. By integrating a dynamic financial dashboard, AI-driven runway forecasting, scenario stress-testing, and an automated investor discovery pipeline (via the OpenVC dataset), STRATIFY gives early-stage founders the tools they need to extend their runway and confidently close their next funding round. Built with React, FastAPI, MongoDB, and Groq LLMs.
